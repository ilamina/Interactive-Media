About Me

My name is Iyanu Lamina. I am originally from Nigeria, but I attend Concordia College, MN, where I am majoring in Computer Science with a concentration in Computing and Data Analytics, and a minor in Web Design and Development. I use she/her pronouns. My favorite food is rice with any kind of meat. I come from a family of eight siblings, and I love my family very much. In Nigeria, there are many ethnic groups with different languages, but the three major ethnic groups are Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa. I am from the Yoruba tribe and was born and raised with a strong understanding of my culture. My interests include cooking and playing sports such as basketball, soccer, and track and field. Although I am not actively involved in sports on campus, I try to engage in these hobbies during my leisure time. Lastly, I love helping people, and I do that best through volunteering work.

I am currently studying to become a data analyst as well as a web designer. However, my passion lies between being a web designer and a data analyst. I love both career paths. In my leisure time, I enjoy coding in HTML and CSS as well as building reports or dashboards in Power BI or Tableau. Outside of class, when I have the chance, I participate in some organizations and clubs on campus. So far, my favorite classes have been Web Design and Development, Software Engineering, and all my data classes: Data Mining, Introduction to Data Analytics, and Applied Statistical Models. These are my favorite classes because I get to work on projects, apply my skills, and grow in this field. Working on projects that interest me excites me and motivates me to work hard. Overall, I appreciate these classes because the topics taught and the skills gained can be applied to real-world problems in the professional or business world.

Definitions
Risk Taker
A risk taker risk taker is someone who is willing to take actions that involve uncertainty or potential loss, often in pursuit of a reward or achievement. They are comfortable stepping out of their comfort zone, accepting the possibility of failure or danger, in order to achieve a goal or experience something new. Risk takers are often associated with traits like boldness, confidence, and resilience.
Problem Solver
A problem solver problem solver is someone who identifies, analyzes, and resolves issues or challenges in a logical and effective manner. They use critical thinking, creativity, and resourcefulness to find solutions, often by breaking down complex situations into manageable parts. Problem solvers are typically proactive, adaptable, and skilled at overcoming obstacles.
Data Analyst
A data analyst data analyst is a professional who collects, processes, and analyzes data to help organizations make informed decisions. They use various tools and techniques to extract insights from data, identify trends, patterns, and relationships, and present findings in a clear, actionable way. Data analysts typically work with large datasets, employing statistical methods, data visualization tools (like Power BI or Tableau), and programming languages (such as SQL or Python) to interpret data and solve business problems. Their role is essential in guiding strategic decisions and improving efficiency within an organization.
